Visualization

Bubble Chart

This bubble chart shows the intensity of the air transport and the ecological footprint of european countries. The year is selected by the time slider above.

Each bubble represents one country. The size corresponds to the average amount of flights per habitant that year. The color shows the ecological footprint of the country as well as the region (North, East, South or West Europe).

Click on a bubble to see the line chart of that country below!

Line Chart

This line chart demonstrates the development of the air transport and the ecological footprint of the selected country (by bubble chart or scatter plot) over time. The data is available from the year 2005 until the year 2016.

The left y-axis shows the values of the average amount of flights per habitant. The right y-axis shows the value of the ecological footprint.

Scatter Plot

This scatter plot allows you to observe the correlation between the values selected above the plot of the year selected in de time slider.

The options for the values are: average amount of flights per habitant, ecological footprint, average amount of train rides (to another European country) per habitant or environmental expenditures by the government (in % of total expenditures).
